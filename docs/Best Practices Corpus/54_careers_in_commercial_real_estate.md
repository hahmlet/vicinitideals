# Careers in Commercial Real Estate
Source: https://www.adventuresincre.com/careers-in-real-estate/
Reading Time: 19 min

The real estate industry is enormous. In the United States alone, over 2.9 million jobs are tied to commercial real estate, 17% of GDP comes from the real estate industry, and the aggregate value of U.S. commercial real estate is approximately $20.7 trillion.

## Career Paths In CRE - Roles, Property Types, and Firm Types

Your value in real estate is largely tied to how many deals you've done, or your "deal seasoning." The more deals you've done, the more weight your assumptions and insights have on future deals. As a result, it's easy to become pigeonholed into a specific geography, property type, and role.

### 1. Real Estate Career Roles

- Academia: Teach real estate at university level
- Acquisitions: Source, analyze, and close existing assets
- Appraisal/Valuation: Approximate a property's value on behalf of third parties
- Asset Management: Manage financial, operational, marketing, and strategic elements of owning RE
- Brokerage: Act as intermediary in RE transactions (CBRE, JLL, Cushman & Wakefield)
- Capital Raising: Raise equity capital for investments or funds
- Construction: Manage the construction process
- Consulting: Provide RE expertise to third parties (PWC, Deloitte, EY, Green Street)
- Corporate Real Estate: Manage RE operations of non-RE companies
- Debt Origination: Raise debt capital (CMBS, Agency, Insurance, Bank, Private)
- Development: Manage the development process (Hines, Trammell Crow, Related)
- Dispositions: Execute strategies for selling RE assets
- Government: Manage RE operations of government entities
- Investment Banking: Enterprise-level RE transactions
- Investor Relations: Manage communications with RE investors
- Loan Servicing: Manage administrative aspects of RE loans
- Portfolio Management: Manage financial and strategic elements of a property portfolio
- Property Management: Day-to-day operations of properties

### 2. Real Estate Property Types

Five major types: Multifamily, Retail, Office, Industrial, Hotel

Sub-types include: Affordable/Market rate housing, Senior housing, Student housing, Freestanding retail, Strip/Neighborhood/Regional centers, CBD/Medical/Suburban office, Flex/Distribution/Manufacturing industrial, Limited/Select/Full service hotels, plus emerging types like Mixed-use, Data centers, Sustainable buildings.

### 3. Real Estate Firm Types

- Limited Partner: Passive partner, brings majority of equity capital (insurance companies, pension funds, endowments, family offices, sovereign wealth)
- General Partner/Sponsor: Finds, acquires, manages investments; brings market expertise
- Advisory/Services: Third-party services to owners or lenders (contractors, appraisers, brokers, property managers)

### Compensation Overview

- Senior executives (20+ years): $250K-$450K base, $500K-$900K total
- Acquisitions: $120K (analyst) to $500K (MD) total
- Asset Management: $150K-$250K (associate) to $550K (MD)
- Property Management: $65K (assistant) to $450K (executive)
- Leasing: $160K to $600K
- Development: $75K (analyst) to $670K (MD)
