### Tool in Action for CRE

**Title:** Fast Market & Deal Screen, From Question to Thesis

**What and Why:** Use RealAI to evaluate a target submarket and a candidate asset side-by-side, benchmark rents/expenses/NOI, see forward-looking risks, and simulate scenarios to decide “pass/pursue/price.” Expect hours saved per deal and more consistent IC narratives.

**Instructions:**

- Search the submarket (city/neighborhood) and review trend cards on values, rent growth, and risk signals; note comps.
- Search the subject property; capture RealAI’s highlights on what makes the asset unique (site, schools, lot, condition) and suggested actions.
- Open the benchmarks panel to compare rents, expenses, and NOI vs. comps; export key figures for your pro forma.
- Run scenario tests (pricing, capex, lease-up) to see sensitivities and stress cases for IC.
- For ongoing oversight, add the asset to monitoring to flag underperformance early and prompt interventions.

**Pro Tip:** Pair RealAI’s market/asset outputs with your deal checklist; its recommendations plus comps streamline the Risks & Mitigations and Why Now sections in IC memos.
